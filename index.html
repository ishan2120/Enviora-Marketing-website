Our Services

Real-Time Vehicle Tracking

Track waste collection vehicles live on an interactive map. See the exact location of collection trucks and know precisely when they'll reach your street for complete transparency.

Automated Push Notifications

Receive timely reminders about upcoming waste collection dates. Stay informed about any schedule changes or if collection trucks fail to arrive in your area.

Collection Schedules

View waste collection schedules tailored specifically to your location. Know exactly when to put out your bins with personalised timetables for your area.

Interactive Segregation Guide

Learn proper waste separation with our visual educational guide featuring pictures and videos. Understand exactly which waste goes into which bin for effective segregation at source.

Special Pickup Requests

Request special collections for bulky items that cannot be accommodated during regular pickups. Dispose of large items conveniently through the platform.

Integrated Reporting System

Report uncollected waste and illegal dumping directly to waste management supervisors through the app. Help maintain cleanliness in your community with just a few taps.

APP

App Interface

Experience a seamless, user-centric interface designed for efficiency. Our app features intuitive navigation, real-time data visualization, and a dark-mode optimized layout to ensure you stay connected to your city's waste management grid with ease.
